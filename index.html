You can filter the visualization by different metrics: views (song bubbles resize by view count, and artist bubbles scale by total views), channel followers (song bubbles stay the same size while artist bubbles scale by subscriber count), duration (song bubbles scale by video length, and artist bubbles reflect total duration), and song genre. Zoom in by clicking an artist to see their songs up close, and zoom out by clicking the background. Hover over any artist or song bubble to view additional details. Overall, this visualization helps you explore popularity, output, and patterns across artists from multiple perspectives.
